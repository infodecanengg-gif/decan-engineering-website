Project Management & Qualification

Decan Engineering provides complete lifecycle project management from concept development to validated commissioning. Our structured approach ensures projects are delivered on time, within budget, and fully compliant with regulatory standards.

Qualification Scope Overview

Phase Description
Design Qualification (DQ) Verification of design intent against URS and regulatory needs
Installation Qualification (IQ) Equipment installation verification as per approved drawings
Operational Qualification (OQ) Functional testing under defined operating conditions
Performance Qualification (PQ) Performance validation under actual production scenarios

  • ✔Risk management and cost control
